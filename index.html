About MY_STUFF

Your Personal Chaos Dashboard // Version 2.0

What is This?

MY_STUFF is a brutalist personal dashboard designed for maximum efficiency with zero fluff. It's your command center for music, radio, games, utilities, and more—all wrapped in a chaotic yet functional interface that refuses to conform to boring design standards.

Built with chaos energy and powered by pure functionality, this dashboard brings together your digital life into one unified space. No bloatware. No tracking. Just raw, unfiltered access to your stuff.

Design Philosophy

We believe in brutalist web design—bold borders, high contrast, intentional asymmetry, and zero pretense. Every element serves a purpose. Every pixel earns its place.

This isn't just another minimalistic white-and-gray dashboard. It's a statement. It's loud, proud, and unapologetically functional. If you wanted boring, you'd be somewhere else.

Privacy First

Your data stays yours. We don't track you. We don't sell your information. We don't load third-party analytics that slow down your experience. This dashboard runs entirely client-side with minimal external dependencies.

Security features include: Content Security Policy headers, XSS protection, input sanitization, secure URL validation, and encrypted connections where applicable. Your privacy isn't an afterthought—it's the foundation.
